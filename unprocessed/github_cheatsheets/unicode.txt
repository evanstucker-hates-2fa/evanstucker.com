Unicode
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Unicode_input
To enter Unicode characters in Linux, type Control-Shift-u followed by the 4 digit hexadecimal code for that character, then hit Enter.
The list of all characters can be found here:
http://www.unicode.org/Public/UCD/latest/ucd/Index.txt
And descriptions are available on Wikipedia:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Unicode_characters
Here are some useful characters:
001f Unit Separator
001e Record Separator
